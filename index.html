About Sabime:
Providing Clean Water Solutions for Nigeria

Our Mission

At Sabime, our mission is simple yet profound: to alleviate the suffering caused by the scarcity of clean and portable water in Nigeria. We believe that every individual deserves access to safe and reliable drinking water, and we are determined to turn this belief into a reality. Our work is guided by the principle of sustainable development, ensuring that the solutions we implement have a lasting impact and empower communities for generations to come.

Our Team

Our dedicated team at Sabime is made up of passionate individuals who share a common vision of a Nigeria where every citizen can access clean water. We have a diverse group of professionals, including engineers, educators, health experts, and community organizers, all working together to make a difference.

Our Impact

Since our inception, Sabime has made a significant difference in the lives of countless Nigerians. We have: Installed hundreds of clean water sources in communities, ensuring access to safe drinking water. Trained thousands of individuals in proper hygiene and sanitation practices, reducing the spread of waterborne diseases. Collaborated with government authorities to advocate for clean water policies, influencing change at a broader scale.
